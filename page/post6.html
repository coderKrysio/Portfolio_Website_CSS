Brick Ball

About

A user-friendly game developed on canvas. The game is played by smashing a wall of bricks by deflecting a bouncing ball with a paddle.

Disclaimer: This project development is an adaptive idea. I do not own any of the imported images or the sound from the online resources. All credits to its rightful owners.

Purpose

To understand the usage of the HTML5 canvas and javascript functions and classes.

Original Idea
